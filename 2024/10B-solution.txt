modified ADFGVX cipher

The square is filled in a spiral:

      A D F G V X
    A l o r d p a
    D c f g h i m
    F b 5 6 7 j e
    G z 4 9 8 k s
    V y 3 2 1 0 t
    X x w v u q n

keyword: LORDPALMERSTON

The labels of the rows and columns are transcribed by a three-needle Cooke-Wheatstone telegraph:

       F
     G   V
   /   \   |
     D   X
       A

These symbols are then transposed in blocks of seven by the permutation (7,3,2,4,6,5,1).
